Carrot Ribbon Salad Recipe

Home

Description

Carrot ribbon salad is a light, refreshing dish that brings out the natural sweetness and crunch of fresh carrots. To make it, large carrots are peeled into thin, wide ribbons using a vegetable peeler, creating a beautiful and elegant base. These ribbons are often tossed with a simple vinaigrette made of olive oil, lemon juice or vinegar, honey or maple syrup, and a touch of salt and pepper. For added flavor and texture, ingredients like fresh herbs (such as parsley or mint), sliced almonds, or crumbled feta cheese can be mixed in.

This salad is perfect as a side dish for warm-weather meals or as a colorful addition to a holiday spread. It's quick to prepare and looks impressive with its vibrant colors and delicate curls. The bright, tangy dressing pairs beautifully with the natural earthiness of the carrots, while the optional toppings add layers of texture and taste. Here's how to make this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 carrots, peeled into wide ribbons using a vegetable peeler (about 4 cups)
  • 1/3 cup crumbled cooked bacon
  • 1/3 cup thinly sliced shallots
  • 3 tablespoons dried sweetened cranberries
  • 3 tablespoons honey-roasted pecan pieces
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, plus more for garnish

Directions to Cook

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk together mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, and salt in a 1-quart container with a lid
  3. Add carrots, bacon, shallots, cranberries, pecan pieces, and parsley; cover and shake until carrots are evenly coated in mayonnaise mixture.
  4. Transfer to a serving bowl and garnish with parsley.
